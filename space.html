Procedural Constellations

Skybox of randomly generated stars and the connections to form random constellations. Utilizing simple graph theory to attempt to mimic the standard western constellations. Meaning to each form is still up to interpretation, much like the stars above us.If no meaning fouund, a new constellation set can be created.

Life on Mars

Nasa has a lot of maps. This one takes multiple mars maps in order to create this render. Clouds were custom procedural generation.

Stary Nights

One of my first 'good' attempts at creating a skybox of stars. Tried my best to mimic old Hubble photos in terms of colors (I did some logarithm tricks). Not really happy with it, the twinkling of stars is a bit much and needs to be slowed and toned down.

Phone controller

This was done as a proof of concept and required a lot of work to be done server-side, more than I ever want to do again. Input lag was also a problem (causing bad flying) again due to server side issues.
