Quaternions

Quaternions are an extensions of the complex numebers to include 3 orthogonal imaginary parts. This means that they have a mandelbrot equivalent.

because quaternions are 4 dimensional, one dimension is used as time and set to animate. setting this constant can lead to good still images.

Quaternions also work with newtons method

quaternions are not commutative (x*y ≠ y*x) so animations and renders are very weird.
